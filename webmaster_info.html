Brad's Poems Page

You can be right, or you can be popular. But you can't be both, at least not always.

You can have your cake, and eat it too. But eventually, something's gotta give.

You can lead a horse to water, but you can't make it drink. Sometimes, no matter what you do, people just won't listen.

You can try to please everyone, but it's a fool's errand. In the end, you'll just end up pleasing no one, including yourself.

You can talk a big game, but it's all hot air. When it comes down to it, you ain't got the goods.
